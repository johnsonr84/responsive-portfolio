    Thanks for being curious about my life.

    I am expanding my skills in Full Stack Development

    I am full of positive energy and enjoy making people laugh. I currently hold a certificate in Front End Development from The Iron Yard and I have 1-year of experience in Front-End Development.

    I am a dogfather to a handsome dogo named Samson

    Samson is my faithful companion and loves the dog park, ice cream, belly rubs and hiking. We both have birthdays in July and we go nearly everwhere together.

    Speedskating is what brought me to Salt Lake City in 2008

    I once pursued a dream of competing in the Olympics. I was occepted onto a high performance team at the Utah Olympic Oval in Kearns, UT. I trained twice a day, five days a week, while being a full time graduate student at The University of Utah studying Music Performance in Percussion. I retired from speedskating in 2010.

    Hockey is one of my favorite sports

    During the winter I play in a local league. Winter sports are kinda my thing and I believe in a healthy balance of work, expercise and personal time. Hockey is a sport I took up after speedskating for many years.

    I have gold fever and seek adventures

    Treasure hunting has become a passion of mine over the years. I was involved with the Forrest Fenn treasure hunt for several years. The treasure was recently found in Wyoming and I have since moved onto other amazing treasure hunts.

    Down by the river is where I can often be found

    Fly fishing is a passion of mine and enjoy spending time on the river and in nature. My favorite states from fly fishing are Montana, Wyoming, Utah and Idaho. I grew up fishing and hunting and I caught my first fish when I was 3 years old and I was hooked!
